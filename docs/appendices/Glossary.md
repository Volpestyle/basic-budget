# Glossary

- **Budget cycle**: The time window used for budgeting (calendar month or biweekly pay period).
- **Period**: A specific instance of a budget cycle (e.g., Feb 2026).
- **Category / Envelope**: A bucket of money intended for a purpose (groceries, rent, etc.).
- **Cadence**: How often a budget amount applies (monthly vs weekly).
- **Carryover / Rollover**: Money remaining (or overspent) that is applied to the next period.
- **Left to spend**: A computed allowance for a time slice (today/week) based on remaining funds and distribution rules.
- **Local-first**: The app works fully offline; local storage is the source of truth.
- **Reconciliation**: Matching imported transactions with categories and avoiding duplicates.
