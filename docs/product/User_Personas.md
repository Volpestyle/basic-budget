# User Personas

## Persona A: “First Budgeter” (Jenna, 26)
- **Context:** New job, wants to stop living paycheck-to-paycheck.
- **Needs:**
  - Quick setup with guidance
  - Simple categories and understandable charts
  - Daily “what can I spend?” number
- **Pain points:**
  - Complex tools overwhelm her
  - Forgetting to track spending

## Persona B: “Routine Budgeter” (Marcus, 34)
- **Context:** Has a stable income and recurring bills; wants tighter control.
- **Needs:**
  - Reliable monthly budgeting with rollovers
  - Alerts when approaching category limits
  - Month-over-month comparisons and trends
- **Pain points:**
  - Needs fast entry and automation
  - Gets annoyed by chart clutter

## Persona C: “Biweekly Planner” (Sam, 29)
- **Context:** Paid biweekly; budgets around paydays.
- **Needs:**
  - Pay-period budgeting with clear “next payday” context
  - Week-based grocery budgets
  - Ability to handle “3-paycheck months” cleanly
- **Pain points:**
  - Monthly budgets don’t match cash flow
  - Wants clarity on available funds between paychecks
